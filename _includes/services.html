Our Mission

To provide a community for exchanging ideas, finding potential partners, understanding the start-up process, and nurturing the next Unicorn.

Innovate

Each semester, our members transform an idea into a viable startup, building unique solutions to crucial problems and developing skills valuable for any student, regardless of one's career interests.

Connect

Through startup workshops, collaboration, socials, and our mentorship platform, we aim to build a community passionate about product development, creating valuable connections and long-lasting relationships.

Aspire

We strive to not only create projects that build off our members' interests, but also provide a substantial social impact and supply a framework for students to develop specialized skills.
